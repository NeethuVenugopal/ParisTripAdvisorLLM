Paris, the capital of France, is a city like no other. Its stunning architecture, rich history, and vibrant culture make it a must-visit destination for travelers from around the world. As your local expert, I'm excited to share with you the ultimate guide to exploring Paris like a true Parisian.

**Getting There**

Paris is easily accessible by air, with two major airports: Charles de Gaulle International Airport (CDG) and Orly Airport (ORY). You can also reach Paris by train from other European cities or by bus from nearby cities.

**Getting Around**

Paris has a comprehensive public transportation system, including the famous métro, buses, and tramways. You can also use taxis, ride-hailing apps, or walk/bike around the city.

**Must-Visit Attractions**

1. Eiffel Tower: The iconic symbol of Paris, offering breathtaking views of the city.
2. Louvre Museum: Home to the Mona Lisa and other world-famous artworks.
3. Notre Dame Cathedral: A beautiful Gothic church, partially destroyed in a fire in 2019.
4. Arc de Triomphe: A monumental arch honoring the soldiers who died in battle.
5. Montmartre: A charming neighborhood with a rich history, street artists, and stunning views.
6. Champs-Élysées: A famous avenue with upscale shopping, dining, and entertainment.
7. Seine River: Take a romantic river cruise or stroll along the riverbanks.

**Parisian Culture and Customs**

1. Respect the locals: Parisians can be a bit formal, so greet them with a "bonjour" or "bonsoir."
2. Dress modestly: Avoid revealing clothing, especially in churches and museums.
3. Learn some French: A few basic phrases like "merci" (thank you) and "s'il vous plaît" (please) will go a long way.
4. Tipping: Leave 5-10% in restaurants and cafes, but not in bars.
5. Siesta: Parisians often take a mid-day break, so you might find shops and restaurants closed between 12:30 pm and 2:30 pm.

**Food and Drink**

1. French cuisine: Indulge in croissants, baguettes, cheese, and wine at a charming café.
2. Markets: Visit the historic Marché aux Puces de Saint-Ouen or the trendy Marché d'Aligre.
3. Chocolate: Treat yourself to a rich, velvety brownie at a patisserie.
4. Coffee: Enjoy a strong espresso or café au lait at a cozy café.

**Safety Tips**

1. Be mindful of pickpocketing in crowded areas and tourist hotspots.
2. Keep an eye on your belongings, especially in public transportation.
3. Avoid walking alone in dimly lit or deserted areas at night.

**Essential Apps**

1. Citymapper: Navigate the Parisian public transportation system.
2. Google Translate: Help you communicate with locals.
3. Parisinfo: Download the official Paris tourist app for maps, attractions, and events.

**Insider Tips**

1. Explore the lesser-known neighborhoods like Le Marais and Belleville.
2. Visit the historic Montmartre Cemetery, where famous artists like Oscar Wilde and Jim Morrison are buried.
3. Enjoy a scenic boat tour along the Seine River.
4. Discover the charming Île de la Cité, with its beautiful gardens and historic landmarks.
